MANAGER GUIDELINES - CONFIDENTIAL

TEAM PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
Managers are responsible for conducting bi-annual performance reviews for all direct reports.
Underperforming employees should be placed on a Performance Improvement Plan after two consecutive poor reviews.
A Performance Improvement Plan must be documented and approved by HR before implementation.
Managers have the authority to recommend salary increases of up to 10 percent for high performers.

HIRING AND RECRUITMENT
Managers can initiate a hiring request through the HR portal with budget approval from Finance.
Interview panels must include at least one HR representative and one senior team member.
All hiring decisions must be documented with clear justification to ensure compliance.
Salary offers must fall within the approved band for the role as defined by HR and Finance.

BUDGET MANAGEMENT
Each department has a quarterly budget allocated at the start of the financial year.
Managers can approve expenses up to 5000 dollars without additional sign-off.
Expenses above 5000 dollars require approval from the department head and Finance.
Budget reports are reviewed monthly and managers must explain variances above 15 percent.

TEAM SALARY INFORMATION
The salary band for junior engineers is 60000 to 80000 dollars per year.
The salary band for senior engineers is 90000 to 120000 dollars per year.
The salary band for team leads is 120000 to 150000 dollars per year.
Annual bonuses range from 5 to 20 percent of base salary depending on performance rating.

DISCIPLINARY PROCEDURES
Verbal warnings must be documented in writing within 24 hours.
Written warnings require HR sign-off before being issued.
Termination decisions require approval from HR and the department head.
All disciplinary actions must be recorded in the employee HR file.
