Q3 FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE
Total company revenue for Q3 reached 47.3 million dollars, up 23 percent year over year.
APAC region contributed 18.2 million dollars representing 38 percent of total revenue.
EMEA region contributed 15.6 million dollars representing 33 percent of total revenue.
Americas region contributed 13.5 million dollars representing 29 percent of total revenue.
Net profit margin improved to 18.4 percent from 14.2 percent in Q3 of the previous year.

ACQUISITION TARGETS
The board has approved exploration of three potential acquisition targets in the AI infrastructure space.
Target A is valued at approximately 120 million dollars with strong recurring revenue of 8 million annually.
Target B is valued at approximately 85 million dollars and would expand our APAC market presence significantly.
Target C is valued at approximately 200 million dollars and represents a transformational acquisition opportunity.
Due diligence on all three targets is expected to complete by end of Q4.
