EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - ACME CORPORATION

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees are entitled to work from home up to 3 days per week. 
Remote work must be approved by your direct manager. 
All remote workers must be available during core hours of 10am to 4pm.
A stable internet connection of at least 50 Mbps is required for remote work.

ANNUAL LEAVE POLICY
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Unused leave can be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days to the next year.
Sick leave is separate from annual leave and requires a medical certificate after 3 consecutive days.

IT EQUIPMENT POLICY
All employees receive a laptop, monitor, keyboard and mouse upon joining.
Equipment must be returned in good condition upon leaving the company.
Personal devices may be used for work but must have company-approved security software installed.
IT support is available Monday to Friday 9am to 6pm via the helpdesk portal.

CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees must treat colleagues with respect and professionalism.
Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated and should be reported to HR immediately.
Confidential company information must not be shared with external parties.
Social media posts about the company must be approved by the communications team.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year in June and December.
Employees should prepare a self-assessment before each review.
Goals are set collaboratively between the employee and their manager.
Performance directly impacts annual salary adjustments and bonus eligibility.
